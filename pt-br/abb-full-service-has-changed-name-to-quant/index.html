ABB Full Service has changed name to Quant

Over the past few months we have gone through a rigorous name selection process, where numerous naming options were considered. Starting with a comprehensive list of over 200 potential names gathered from internal and external sources, we narrowed it down to a shortlist of eight relevant and appealing names. The names on the shortlist were then further evaluated in terms of their appeal, usability, uniqueness and strategic fit. The detailed search and evaluation led to one clear winner: Quant.

Our new name reflects the quantifiable value, professionalism and expertise we offer our customers on a day-to-day basis. It furthermore emphasizes our data-driven and sophisticated approach to industrial maintenance management. Even though our name is changing, our focus is not. From improving safety and reducing cost, to taking performance to the next level, our people will continue to make all the difference for our customers.
